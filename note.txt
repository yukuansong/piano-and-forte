Piano Pals is an afterschool music and mentoring program currently offered in select Montgomery County Public Schools that provides disadvantaged elementary school students with a middle school or high school mentor who helps the younger child, who would otherwise not be able to afford lessons, learn music. 

Who are we?
Piano & Forte is an afterschool music and mentoring program currently offerred in the select Prince George County Public Schools that provides disadvantaged elementary school students with a UMD mentor who helps the youner child who would otherwise not be able to afford lessons, learn music.

Who can join us as volunteer teacher?
If you're a student looking for a meaningful way to earn volunteer hours or piano teaching experience, this opportunity is for you!
